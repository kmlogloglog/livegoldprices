About Live Gold Prices

Free, real-time gold price data for everyone

Our Mission

Live Gold Prices was created with a simple goal: to provide free, accurate, and real-time gold price data to anyone in the world. Whether you're an investor monitoring your portfolio, a jeweler checking daily rates, or simply curious about the gold market, our platform delivers the information you need in a clean, fast, and beautiful interface.

Gold has been valued by civilizations for thousands of years. It remains one of the most important financial assets globally, serving as a store of value, an inflation hedge, and a safe-haven investment. We believe that access to real-time gold pricing data should be free and available to everyone — not locked behind paywalls or cluttered with distracting advertisements.

What We Offer

Our dashboard provides a comprehensive suite of tools for tracking the gold market:

  • Real-Time Gold Prices: Live gold spot prices updated every 30 seconds, displayed in 8 major currencies — USD, EUR, GBP, CHF, AUD, CAD, JPY, and INR.
  • Interactive Charts: Professional-grade TradingView charts with multiple timeframes (24-hour, 7-day, 30-day, and 1-year views) to analyze price movements and trends.
  • Weight Converter: Instantly convert gold prices between troy ounces, grams, kilograms, and tolas in any supported currency.
  • Market Data: Key metrics including 24-hour high and low, opening price, price range, bid-ask spread, and trading volume estimates.
  • Live Market Ticker: A scrolling ticker tape showing real-time prices for gold, silver, major indices, top stocks, forex pairs, and Bitcoin.
  • Educational Blog: In-depth articles covering gold investing, price history, market factors, and practical guides for buyers.
  • Market News: Aggregated gold and precious metals news to keep you informed about events that move the market.

Our Data Sources

Accuracy and reliability are at the core of what we do. We source our data from trusted, industry-standard providers:

  • GoldPrice.org: Our primary source for real-time gold spot prices in USD and EUR. GoldPrice.org aggregates data from major global markets and provides reliable, continuously updated pricing.
  • TradingView: We use TradingView's professional-grade charting widgets to display interactive price charts, ticker tapes, and market timelines. TradingView is trusted by millions of traders and investors worldwide.
  • Frankfurter API: Our currency conversion engine is powered by the Frankfurter API, which provides exchange rates published by the European Central Bank. This ensures accurate and up-to-date currency conversions for gold prices across all supported currencies.

Gold prices are refreshed automatically every 30 seconds. Currency exchange rates are updated every 5 minutes. All data is indicative and should not be used as the sole basis for financial decisions.
